Al Asema Contracting

Complete brand identity system for a construction and contracting company in Egypt, designed to reflect strength, reliability, and professionalism.

Al Asema Contracting operates within Egypt’s construction sector, requiring a visual identity that communicates trust, structural strength, and long-term credibility.

The logo was designed to convey balance, stability, and precision. Its structure allows it to scale across construction signage, uniforms, and corporate materials without losing clarity.

The visual identity system ensures consistency across all brand touchpoints. Typography, color usage, and supporting elements were selected to align with the construction industry’s expectations.

On-site photography was captured to connect the brand identity with real construction environments, reinforcing authenticity, scale, and execution.
